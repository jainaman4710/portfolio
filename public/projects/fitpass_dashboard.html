Proposed Solutions

Personalisation + Gamification
Data-driven workout & diet plans with a weekly streak league system to boost long-term retention.
League progression system
Silver Weeks 1–4
Gold Weeks 4–8
Diamond Weeks 8–12

Users maintain streaks by completing weekly workout goals (e.g., 4 of 5 planned workouts). Streaks reset every 3 months; seasonal resets keep competition fresh and allow newcomers to enter competitively.

Key features
  • User profile & initial registration questionnaire (age, goals, fitness level, preferences)
  • Customised workout & diet plans based on gym/home preference and macronutrient goals
  • Feedback collection via manual rating + fitness tracker integration (calories, heart rate)
  • Weekly progress reports: workouts completed, calories burned, streak maintained
  • Community & social sharing: create groups, share badges within FITPASS Groups

Campaign goals
Increase user engagement
Encourage FITPASS users to actively participate in fitness challenges, post on social media, and interact with the community.
Acquire new users
Attract new users to the FITPASS app through the viral nature of social media challenges and influencer engagement.
Drive premium subscriptions
Use competition and community buzz to promote premium features and convert free users to paid subscriptions.
Eligibility & guardrails
  • Open to all FITPASS users aged 16+ residing in India
  • FITPASS employees and immediate family ineligible for rewards
  • Only videos from public accounts counted for likes
  • Automated bots/systems → immediate disqualification
  • One person can only redeem top-place rewards once per year
  • Existing premium users can extend duration with winning reward
