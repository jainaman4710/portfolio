Risk and mitigation
Key risks, legal constraints, and how we address them
01 / LEGAL AND REGULATORY
Age verification and jurisdictional compliance
Multiple jurisdictions require strict age verification for adult content. Mitigation: robust age-gating, continuous monitoring, region-specific compliance frameworks.
02 / CONTENT MODERATION
AI-generated prohibited content
AI may inadvertently generate content involving minors, non-consensual scenarios, or violence. Mitigation: multi-layer automated filters and human review for high-risk categories including minor content, incest, and non-consensual material.
03 / PAYMENT PROCESSORS
Major processors restrict adult businesses
Stripe, PayPal, and similar processors routinely restrict adult content platforms. Mitigation: build relationships with adult-industry-friendly financial institutions and payment processors from day one.
04 / TECHNICAL AND SECURITY
Data privacy breaches
Intimate user data is high-value and high-sensitivity. Mitigation: end-to-end encryption, zero-trust data architecture, and regular third-party security audits.
Legal constraints overview
Consent and privacy: Ensure proper consent for training data to avoid privacy violations.
Intellectual property: Incorporate human creative input to strengthen copyright claims.
Liability and responsibility: Implement robust content moderation to prevent misuse.
Regulatory compliance: Stay updated on evolving regulations across different jurisdictions.
Deepfakes and non-consensual content: Prohibit and actively detect attempts to create deepfakes of real individuals.
Platform and tool access restrictions: Develop proprietary technologies or partner with industry-friendly providers.
Child protection laws: Implement strict age verification and AI filters to prevent CSAM.
Financial discrimination: Build relationships with adult-industry-friendly financial institutions.
