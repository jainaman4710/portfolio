Executive Overview  |  Root Cause Analysis
Why Users Aren't Coming Back
Despite 30% market share and 50M+ app downloads, Nykaa faces three structural challenges driving user churn and low repeat purchase frequency.
🚪

High First-Order Drop-Off

A large proportion of new users disengage after their first purchase. The onboarding experience lacks personalisation and fulfilment inconsistencies undermine early trust.

Root Cause: Unpersonalised onboarding + fulfillment failures
🔁

Low Repeat Purchase Frequency

Repeat purchase rates remain low despite regular discount pushes. Loyalty benefits and replenishment options are not tailored or automated, reducing motivation to return.

Root Cause: Generic loyalty + no automated replenishment
📱

Limited App Engagement

Nykaa's app is highly transactional with minimal reason to open it outside of shopping intent. Content and community features are siloed from the core shopping experience.

Root Cause: Siloed content + no community integration

Navigation Wizard

AI-powered natural language search and discovery
RICE Score 64.0  |  #2 Priority

Users type a natural language query; the system performs intent recognition, slot filling, and dynamic query generation to surface perfectly filtered results. Directly tackles the overwhelm caused by 200,000+ SKUs and aligns with the Smart Navigator pain point across all three user personas.

Rollout
P1: Query Parsing + Intent Engine P2: Filter Mapping + Ranking P3: Local Cache + Feedback Loop
↓ Bounce Rate ↑ AOV ↑ New User Engagement 40% Adoption Target

Video-Based Reviews

Nykaa Play integrated directly into product pages

Product pages link to curated video reviews on Nykaa Play, increasing trust, boosting influencer visibility, and creating a networking flywheel that brings creators and buyers closer to the product. The highest RICE score of all four features due to low effort and high confidence.

Rollout
P1: Link products to available reviews P2: Grow community features P3: Video reviews on majority of SKUs

🌿 What is Nykaa Organic?

03

Organic Filter & Badge

Nykaa-verified organic product badging system
RICE Score 45.0  |  #3 Priority

Nykaa verifies and badges organic products with a standardised green label, visible in search results and product pages. Addresses the 71% of Indian consumers who want natural options, while giving Nykaa a trust differentiator over horizontal marketplaces like Amazon.

Rollout
P1: Verify and badge popular products P2: Speed up broader verification P3: Full rollout with built-in awareness
↑ Organic Filter Usage ↑ Ingredient Trust ↑ New Segment Capture 30% Usage Target
04

Image-Based Try-On

Upload a photo, see the product on your face
RICE Score 24.0  |  #4 Priority (Long-term)

Users upload a photo; face detection and makeup overlay renders realistic product shades instantly. Designed to work on low-camera-quality devices too, expanding accessibility significantly. Photos are end-to-end encrypted and auto-deleted after 48 hours, with clear consent flows throughout.
