Metrics and Success Framework

How We Measure Success

North Star Metric, scale-up metrics, and adoption and retention KPIs for PolicyEase.

North Star Metric
Policy Renewals and Claims Processed per User
= (Number of policies renewed per month) x (Number of claims processed per user in a month)
Scale-Up Metric
Sustainability Index
(Customer Retention Rate) x (Policy Renewal Rate) / Customer Acquisition Cost
Tracks the platform's ability to retain users, renew policies, and manage acquisition costs effectively.
Scale-Up Metric
Adoption Rate
(New Users per Month) / (Total Target Users) x 100
Tracks the platform's pace of gaining new users relative to the total addressable market size.
Adoption and Growth
User Growth Rate
(Total Users at Beginning of Period) / (New Users in Period) x 100
Critical indicator of the app's success in gaining traction, especially important in early phases.
Engagement and Retention
MAU / DAU Ratio
(Active Users in Month or Day) / Total Registered Users x 100
Measures the app's ability to keep users engaged over time, a key health indicator for insurance apps.
Renewals
Primary engagement loop
Policies renewed monthly x claims per user
Retention
Sustainability index driver
CRR x PRR divided by CAC
Trust
Long-term moat metric
CSAT, NPS, transparent claims score
