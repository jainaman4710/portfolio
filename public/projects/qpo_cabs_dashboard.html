Women Safety Framework

Empowering Community Through Safety

QPo Cabs builds trust with female riders through a layered safety system, from nominee monitoring to an exclusive women-driver program.

👤
QPo Nominee
Trusted person receives real-time ride updates and emergency alerts
📡
Real-Time Monitoring
Live GPS + geofencing detects route deviations and auto-alerts nominee
🚨
Emergency SOS
Panic button alerts nearest police station; hidden SOS mode known only to user
🚕
Women at the Wheel
Exclusive women-driver program empowering female drivers to join the transport sector
Safety Feature Deep Dive
👤
QPo Nominee System
Riders register a trusted relative or friend as their nominee. During rides, the nominee receives real-time location updates. Geofencing triggers automatic alerts for route deviations, providing constant oversight without active monitoring.
📡
Live GPS & Geofencing
Advanced real-time tracking monitors the driver's exact route. Any deviation from the planned path instantly notifies both the nominee and the platform operations team for rapid response.
🚨
Emergency SOS Button
In-app panic button directly connects to the nearest police station. A secondary hidden SOS feature (visible only to the rider) enables discreet activation in high-risk scenarios without alerting the driver.
🛡️
Driver Training & Community Partnerships
All drivers undergo safety training including risk and fatigue management. QPo partners with local communities and authorities to build awareness and maintain accountability standards.
Safety Benefits for QPo's Business
Customer Loyalty: Safety-first reputation turns female riders into repeat users and active brand advocates.
Market Differentiation: Women-only rides and nominee system distinguishing QPo from Ola Share and Uber Pool; neither offers these.
Regulatory Goodwill: Proactive safety measures build positive relationships with local police and municipal authorities.
Driver Supply: Women-at-the-Wheel program unlocks an underutilised driver segment, expanding fleet supply.

→ Multiple payment options + offline payment backup system

→ Automated lost-and-found tracking system built into the app

Brand Building in Chennai

🧻 Wet Wipes Co-Branding Campaign
Why Wet Wipes in Chennai?
Chennai's tropical climate (avg. 30°C year-round) creates real hygiene needs for daily commuters. QPo Labs partners with a local FMCG brand to place QPo-branded wet wipe packets across distribution channels. Barter model: with zero cash outlay.
🤝

🏪
In-Store Promotional Materials
